Okay! You already know that from last 3 days I have been questioning god about my purpose for this life. Suddenly just after two days of questioning yesterday morning, I had a dream in which I was getting tensed about ESE exam(in my dream it was to happen when I wake up). Now generally I don't remember my dreams when I wake up. But this dream got somehow registered. May be it is not the purpose for which I was born. But it can for sure be my path to fulfill the purpose.

It I observe, from February after GATE exam I only did some newspaper reading. But surprisingly the first thing I started with was mechanical engineering optional. When ever I started with another subject(ethics), I felt desolated and was unable to enjoy things completely. Of course it would have been beautiful if I would have initially started with history, geography etc subjects. But I did otherwise. May be because of that I felt desolated.

I have thinking about this from yesterday. As I'm already done with 75% of the syllabus my feeling is that I can easily do with ESE. Now the questions are:

  • Should I go with ESE because I feel it is easy or because I really want to do it?
  • I'm unable to answer the above question. But if I'm doing it because it is easy, what is the guarantee that I will not finally stick with GATE as I did last year?
  • Okay what after ESE? Will I be satisfied with ESE job, no I will feel like doing CSE again. So why not continue with CSE itself. Even if I fail, I will atleast get experience to try again.
  • Should I instead continue with CSE and now do it other way - start with history, geography etc?
  • I really enjoyed the CSE journey, but the problem was note making, newspaper which consumed a lot of time. Should I find the mistake I'm doing again and start again?
  • CSE for me is of medium toughness, but I know that it requires 100% discipline. The first thing that I cannot stop thinking is how will I manage ill-disciplined family?
  • Everything while about to take a decision seems very easy, is ESE decision also the same?
  • One reason I didn't like ESE was its prelims paper 1. How will I continue with that syllabus?
  • Another thing is ESE only has ~5 months of time. What is the guarantee that I will not feel frustrated during these days and show my anger to the family? Which will make me loose few days and finally I will only settle with GATE!!!!!!!
  • Why ESE why not CSE? The only answer I'm hearing is because it is easy and you already have experience of sources, strategy etc.
  • One thing is guarantee, attitude of a civil servant is needed for both CSE and ESE. If you are able to do ESE you should be able to do CSE. Then why not do CSE where I can serve people directly and see their happiness?

Now while I write this blog post, I feel like I have to stick with CSE only. I think this is the best way. I want to go to universal book store and purchase the history, goegraphy etc books and do a restart.

You know this is the reason I wanted to write a blog post. It will help me make a simple and easy decision.
